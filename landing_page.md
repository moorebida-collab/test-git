## HOW TO GET YOUR MAILCHIMP FORM ACTION URL

Follow these steps in your Mailchimp account (Moorebida@gmail.com):

1. Log into **mailchimp.com**
2. In the left sidebar click **Audience → All contacts**
3. Click **Signup forms** (top right of the audience page)
4. Click **Embedded forms**
5. Copy the URL inside the `action="..."` attribute of the `<form>` tag — it looks like:
   `https://gmail.us21.list-manage.com/subscribe/post?u=XXXXXXXX&amp;id=XXXXXXXX`
6. Paste that URL in place of `YOUR_MAILCHIMP_FORM_ACTION_URL` in the script above
7. Remove `&amp;` and replace with `&` if needed

## SETTING UP THE MMERGE3 TAG FIELD IN MAILCHIMP

The hidden field `MMERGE3` passes the user's selection (app-signup / book-buyer / both) into Mailchimp so you can tag and route them to the right email sequence.

**To create this field in Mailchimp:**
1. Go to **Audience → Settings → Audience fields and *|MERGE|* tags**
2. Click **Add a field** → choose **Text**
3. Label it: `Journey Path`
4. Set the tag to: `MMERGE3`
5. Click **Save**

Once this field exists, every form submission will populate it with the user's selection, and your Customer Journey automation can use it to trigger Sequence A (app) or Sequence B (book).
